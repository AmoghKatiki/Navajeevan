Our Mission: New Life (Navajeevan)

Education First

We ensure every girl has access to quality schooling, providing textbooks, notebooks, and tutoring support to foster a love for learning.

Year-Round Support

From monsoon raincoats to warm winter blankets, we adapt our aid to meet the seasonal and daily needs of the children.

Long-Term Growth

Beyond immediate needs, we fund upgrades and workshops to ensure long-term stability and empowerment.

Our Current Seasonal Focus

Monsoon Protection (Umbrellas & Raincoats)

The Odisha monsoon is intense. Donations help us buy umbrellas and raincoats so girls can attend school safely.

Academic Essentials (Textbooks & Supplies)

We’re raising funds for new textbooks and school supplies for the next academic year.
