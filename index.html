Empowering Odisha’s Girls Through Education

Navajeevan provides safety, schooling, and support to young girls affected by poverty, displacement, and natural disasters.

Join the Cause

Our Mission

Navajeevan is dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged girls in Odisha by offering education, healthcare, and mentorship. We believe that empowering one girl uplifts an entire community.

Our Impact

150+ Girls Educated

Providing access to formal schooling and digital literacy tools.

10 Villages Reached

Expanding to rural and disaster-affected regions of Odisha.

30 Mentors Engaged

Connecting professionals and college students with young learners.
