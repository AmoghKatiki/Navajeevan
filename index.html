Our Mission: New Life (Navajeevan)

Education First

We ensure every girl has access to quality schooling, providing essential resources like textbooks, notebooks, and tutoring support to foster a love for learning.

Year-Round Support

From monsoon season protection (umbrellas, raincoats) to winter blankets, we adapt our aid to meet the seasonal and daily needs of the children.

Long-Term Growth

Beyond immediate needs, we aim to fund facility upgrades and skill-building workshops, ensuring the orphanage is a place of lasting stability and growth.

Our Current Seasonal Focus

Monsoon Protection (Umbrellas & Raincoats)

The monsoon season in Odisha is intense. Your donation helps purchase durable umbrellas and raincoats to ensure the girls can safely travel to and from school and stay dry.

Academic Essentials (Textbooks & Supplies)

Education is paramount. We are currently collecting funds for new textbooks and essential school supplies to prepare the girls for the next academic year.
