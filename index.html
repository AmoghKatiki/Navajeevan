Our Mission

Navajeevan—meaning “New Life”—was started to support a girls’ orphanage in Odisha, India. Our mission is to ensure every girl receives the resources, education, and care she needs to grow and thrive. We aim to make sure their home remains a safe, nurturing, and empowering environment year-round.

Through donations, we provide essentials for school, clothing, and seasonal needs, while also funding long-term projects to improve living conditions and create sustainable opportunities.

Our Current Seasonal Focus

Monsoon Protection

During monsoon season, we provide raincoats, umbrellas, and waterproof shoes so the girls can safely walk to school and stay dry.

Academic Essentials

We are collecting funds to purchase new textbooks and school supplies for the upcoming academic term.
